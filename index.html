National Gingerbread Contest

Ann Bailey

1st in Adult Category

From Cary, North Carolina, Ann Bailey won top honors with “When Dreams Have Wings,” a magical mix of Peter Pan and Santa Claus themes.

Beatriz Muller

2nd Place in Adult Category

Beatriz Muller from Cold Water, Ontario, earned second place for her elegant clock-inspired piece called “The Time of Christmas Past.

Tiny Farm Bakers

3rd Place in Adult Category

The Tiny Farm Bakers from Montreat, North Carolina, took third with “Merry Mountain Mayhem,” showing tall pine trees and a cozy log cabin.

Rhinehart Girls

1st Place in Teen Category (Ages 13–17)

From Columbia, Tennessee, the Rhinehart Girls won first in the teen group for their creative “Fairy Tea House,” a fun and magical tea set.

Coble Adams

1st Place in Youth Category (Ages 9–12)

Coble Adams from Raleigh, North Carolina, topped the youth group with “Santa Yoda and His Baby Elves,” a Star Wars-themed gingerbread creation.

Maisy Frahler

1st Place – Child Category (Ages 5–8)

Maisy Frahler from Greenville, South Carolina, won first place in the child group with her adorable “Christmas Morning Paper Dolls.
